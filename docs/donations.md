# Donating to TAK

TAK is free and open source (MIT). If it saves you time, please consider supporting continued development.

---

## Credit Card

Donate via **[GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/lchonkan)**.

- All major credit and debit cards accepted
- GitHub charges no fees on sponsorships — 100% goes to the maintainer
- One-time or recurring monthly amounts
